The Soup Kitchen

Our mission is simple: to feed people in need regardless of their circumstances. The Soup Kitchen provides healthy and nutritious meals to all who come through our doors. We are committed to improving the quality of life and restoring the dignity of those we serve when providing a hot meal to any member of our community in need of one.

Loaves and Fishes Soup Kicthen

Loaves and Fishes Soup Kitchen was organized in February 1983 at Child's Restaurant in Pensacola, Florida. For 30 years, our goal has been to reach out to homeless men, women and children in an effort to meet their needs both spiritually and materially.

The Volunteer Way

The Volunteer Way Inc. (TVW) is a 501(c)(3), faith base non-profit organization that is dedicated to helping those in need – children, seniors and adults caught in poverty, the hungry, the homeless, low income individuals and their families. For over twenty three years we have been committed to assisting the needy in our community.
