In the state of New Jersey there have been over 8,941 person who are homeless. Most of people who have become homeless had a job and a stable residence before anything had happened. The reason for why they were homeless was because there was a rapid change in their economic and social factors. Not everybody becomes homeless because they may have lost their jobs or of the rapid change is their economic/social factors. They also become homeless because they may struggle with substance abuse, have a mental illness, fleeing from domestic violence, or their veterans. Since they have some type of struggle they are most likely kicked out of their home.
